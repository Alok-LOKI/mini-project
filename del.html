Inactiveivery charges for orders from the Online Shop?

We charge rule based optimized Inactiveivery charge on each order, you can check that at the time of checkout of your order. In most cases it is ₹99-/ only.

How long will Inactiveivery take?

All orders are Inactiveivery by Road or Train Transportation. Generally we dispatch orders withing 2-3 working days and it takes 5-6 days to reach your place depending on Transpiration feasibility

Call Before Inactiveivery

By requesting a FREE call Before Inactiveivery the carrier will contact you approximately 24 hours before the estimated arrival of your order to schedule a Inactiveivery appointment using the phone number provided at checkout.
Selecting this option may add an additional day to your order’s transit time.
A 4-hour Inactiveivery window is typical; this may vary by carrier and shipping destination.
A Call Before Inactiveivery is required for residential Inactiveiveries and recommended for businesses with irregular hours (Monday through Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
Call Before Inactiveivery only applies to the item(s) in your order shipping via common carrier.
If the carrier misses the Inactiveivery appointment they will reschedule it.
If you are unavailable to accept your Inactiveivery during the scheduled appointment, you will be responsible for any reInactiveivery fees owed to the carrier. Additional fees will apply if you require a time-specific Inactiveivery in which the typical 4-hour window needs to be shortened. If a Inactiveivery is unable to be completed because a “Call Before Inactiveivery” was not selected during checkout, the carrier may charge a re-Inactiveivery fee in order to reattempt Inactiveivery. In these situations, we will contact you via email regarding the fee and charge the card on file if necessary in order to avoid additional fees with the carrier.
